    \subsection{Level of service: costs and
benefits}\label{level-of-service-costs-and-benefits}

This scoring component currently is written as:
\[S_{unscaled,i} = \frac{F_i(C_{BAU})}{F_i(C_s)}\] where \(C\)
represents the monetary benefits minus the monetary costs.

\textbf{Note}: \emph{The submission metric is in the denominator}

Due to the fact that both numerator and denominator can take negative or
zero values, this functional form is problematic:

\begin{enumerate}
\def\labelenumi{\arabic{enumi})}
\tightlist
\item
  When the denominator is close to zero, the scoring component is
  discontinuous and tends towards positive and negative infinity.
\item
  When the numerator is negative, minimization pushes the solution
  towards a slightly less profitable situation.
\end{enumerate}
